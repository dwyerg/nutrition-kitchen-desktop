About Us

Why Nutrition?

We know how hard it can be to get started on a nutritious path, which is why we decided to develop this website full of resources that will aide in your transition to a healthier lifestyle. Because there have been countless others before you we have refined our craft to make it as accessible and easy for you as possible. Please take your time looking around, there is quite a lot to see and we don't want you to miss one bit of it.

Our Mission and Values

Mission: To simplify what it means to be healthy so this lifestyle can be accessible to more people. Many people grew up without little to no guidance when it comes to making healthy choices. They were victims of their circumstance and we want to rectify the overall situation.

Values: Unhealthy eating is systematic and problematic which is why we are so passionate about the subject. For decades, people have resigned themsleves to eating fast food thinking it is the easiest and cheapest choice. And while they aren't entirely incorrect, switching to a healthier lifestyle doesn't necessarily mean a huge jump in the cost or accessiblity of food.

Our Founders

We have two main founders of the Nutrition Kitchen: the sisters Crystal and Diamond Johnson. They grew up in a small town in Michigan, eating mac and cheese on the daily. Once they moved away to collge, they realized that their way of eating didn't align with the healthiest choices. They both decided to major in nutrition and upon graduation, they opened up their company: Nutrition Kitchen.
