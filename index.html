Nutrition Kitchen

Welcome to Nutrition Kitchen! We are so glad you decided to stop by. Our goal is to help you create the healthiest kitchen possible while still being able to enjoy the food you are making. By improving your pantry to contain all the essentails and increasing the nutritious recipes in you arsenal, we hope you will begin to enjoy your time in the kitchen.

Many people struggle to develop the habit of cooking every meal, especially if they are college students. They find they don't have enough time to prep and cook a meal, let alone all of the clean up afterwards. We aspire to bring you easy recipes that you can throw on the stove or in the oven and forget about it until you are hungry. Many of our dishes are even advertised as being 3 bowls or less, making it easy to throw all your dishes in one load of the dishwasher. Additionally, we have have a section in our product recomendations where we discuss several groups of spices you can use on a daily basis without feeling like you've needed to spend hundreds of dollars to make your food taste good.

Eating healthy doesn't need to be a drag on your mental health or on your wallet. We want you to do what's best for your body, while on a reasonable budget. In many of our recipes we provide alternatives if you are looking to keep things on the cheaper side.

Lastly, we want to aknowledge how difficult it can be to make the transiton to cooking on the daily and focusing on your health. It is encouraged for you to slowly ease into using our recipes and transforming our shopping habbits. This can be especially hard if you are strictly adhere to our products and recipes since they are all gluten free and mostly vegetarian. These categoreies aren't necessary for ultimate health but we know many people struggle with dietary restictions and having these accomodations would be helpful for many.

Now go have fun with the resources we have to offer! Feel free to contact us if you have any questions!
