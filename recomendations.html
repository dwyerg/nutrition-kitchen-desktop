Nutritious Products

Essential Spices

Garlic Powder
This makes a great addition to any spice cabinet. You can add it to anything and it saves you the hassle of having to mince garlic cloves up. Similarly, adding onion powder to you regular rotation would be an excellent idea as well. Both of these are staples in almost any dish.

Cumin
Cumin is one spice you never want to run out of. Although it is not commonly used for most people, we find that throwing it into most savory dishes really accentuates the other flavors of the dish and add a nice earthy overtone.

Nutmeg
On the opposite end of the spectrum is nutmeg, which is more classically used in desserts or other sweet dishes. Add nutmeg to you oatmeal in the morning or simply spice up a batch of cookies for an extra hint of fall spices. Many people resort to cinnamon, but the flavor profile with both spices makes for a nice warm treat.

Necessary Basics

Fruit
As any health nut will tell you, fruit is an excellent snack. It is super low calorie and will satisfy any sweet tooth. We suggest you always keep a bowl of fruit on the counter (or in the refridgerator if you have fruit flies). You can populate it with apples, plums, bananas and our personal favorite, avocados.

Pasta
Feeling like you don't have time to cook a complicated dish? Making any sort of pasta is easy and fast. We recomend protein pasta, such as chickpea or lentil pasta, to boost the heartiness of the meal. You can use multiple kinds of sauces to garnish: marinara, alfredo, pesto, and so much more.

Protein Powder
In the morning, people often want a dish that is quick any easy to make. We often say that smoothies are the best bet, you can even prepare them the night before without much hassle. Throw in the fruit we mentioned earlier, along with a decent helping of protein powder and you have an extremely filling and healthy breakfast.

Fun Alternatives

Firm Tofu
If you have a little more flex in your budget, we suggest picking up a pack of firm tofu the next time you're at the grocery store. While it does have a large amount of soy, the protein content is very high as well. You can whip up tofu cubes easily in a well oiled plan or in a newfangled air fryer.

Vegan Burger Patties
For those of you who prefer the meatier things in life, you can easily use beef patties as well. However, because we are keeping things as vegetarian, we bring up veggie patties as an excellent alterantive. You don't even need to assmble a whole burger, just cut up the patty with some ketchup and chow down. Plus, they're delicious!

Ice Cream
We know that it can be hard to eat cleanly all of the time, which is why we are including ice cream on this list of products. Everything is okay in moderation. We suggest a small scoop of vanilla ice cream (non-dairy optional) with some blueberries or strawberries sprinkled on top. Yum!!
